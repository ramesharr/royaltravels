Tamilnadu temple package

Package Starts from 12500/Rs

ITINERARY

Day 01 : Chennai

Arrive Chennai @ 07.45 Hrs. meet and transfer to hotel, Proceed for half day city tour covering : Kapaleshwarar, Temple, Parthasarathy Temple, Government Museum, Vallauvar Kottam, Marina Beach. Overnight stay at Chennai

Day 02 : Chennai – Kancheepuram – Mahabalipuram

Breakfast, Check out hotel and drive to Kancheepuram. On arrival visit the Ekambareshwarar Temple, Vaikuntha Perumal Temple. Then proceed to Mahabalipuram. Check in Beach Resort. Evening visit the Shore Temple, Pancha Ratha’s , Arjuna’s Penance. Over night stay at Mahabalipuram.

Day 03 : Mahabalipuram - Pondicherry

Breakfast, check out hotel and drive to Pondicherry. On arrival check in hotel. Evening visit Auroville, Shri Aurobindo Ashram, Manakula Vinayagar Temple, French Colonial town. Overnight stay at Pondicherry.

Day 04: Pondicherry – Chidambaram – Vaitheeswaran Koil – Thanjavur

Breakfast, check out hotel and drive to Chidambaram, Enroute visit the Vaitheeswaran Koil. On arrival visit the Natarajar Temple. Then proceed to Thanjavur. Check in hotel. Evening visit the Lord Brahadeeshwarar Temple. Overnight stay at Thanjavur.

Day 05 : Thanjavur – Kumbakonam - Thanjavur

Breakfast, proceed to visit the Kumbakonam Temples, GangaiKonda Cholapuram, Dharasuram. Overnight stay at Thanjavur

Day 06 : Thanjavur – Rameshwaram

Breakfast, check out hotel and drive to Rameshwaram. On arrival check hotel Royal Park. Holy dip in Sea, Agni Teertha Snanam, Visit the Ramanatha Swamy Temple. Overnight stay at Rameshwaram

Day 07: Rameshwaram – Kanyakumari

Breakfast, check out hotel and drive to Kanyakumari. On arrival check in hotel. Evening visit the Vivekananda Rock Memorial, Kumari Amman Temple, Sunset View. Overnight stay at Kanyakumari.

Day 08 : Kanyakumari – Madurai

Breakfast, check out hotel and drive to Madurai. On arrival check in hotel. Proceed for half day city tour covering : Thiruparakundram Temple, 1000 Pillar Hall, Naicker Mahal, Then Famous Meenakshi Amman Temple. Overnight stay at Madurai.

Day 09 : Madurai – Palani – Trichy

Breakfast, check out hotel and drive to Trichy. Enroute visit the Palani Andavar Temple. Check-in hotel. Evening visit the Rock Fort Temple, Sri Ranganathar Temple. Overnight stay at Trichy.

Day 10: Trichy –Thiruvannamalai

Breakfast, check out hotel and drive to Thiruvannamalai. On arrival visit the Arunchaleeshwarar Temple, Ramanamaharishi Ashram. Overnight stay at Thiruvannamalai.

Day 11: Thiruvannamalai – Vellore – Tirupathi

Breakfast, check out hotel and drive to Tirupathi. Enroute visit the Vellore Fort, Shiva Temple. On arrival at Tirupathi check-in hotel. Overnight stay at Tirupathi.

Day 12: Tirupathi – Chennai

Morning Balaji Darshan, afternoon check out the hotel, drive to Chennai. (Evening free for shopping) Check-in hotel Overnight stay at Chennai.

Day 13: Chennai

Early morning check out the hotel, drop at airport. back to home.
